Graduation Day!

chapter in my life. I wasn't sure if my friends would keep in
touch and I was nervous but excited about living in a new city.

not hit me that I was leaving high school so while eveyone was
sentimental I was treating this like any regular high school
assembly. This was during the pandemic so this photo is the
first time we were all together in a while.
