College Memories

First Year of
College!

I think I had such a nice experience for the first year
of college. Being in a new city from where I grew
was a big change but I think it is the change I
needed. San Francisco is an amazing city with so
much to do compared to Portland, Oregon. It feels
like I can never get bored of this city, Truly I
think I fell in love with this city.

Met some
great people

I still think its crazy how many people I have met in
the past year. My roommate that was assigned to
me is one of my closest friends. I was so nervous
about starting college classes but literally found
two friends thew moment I showed up to class.
I have so many memories from my first year of
college that I hold so much happiness for that
school year.
